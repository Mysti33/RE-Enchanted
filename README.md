**RE:Enchanted** is a Minecraft mod that adds a collection of unique and powerful enchantments to enhance gameplay. From weapons that heal you to boots that let you walk on lava, this mod makes your adventures more magical and fun.

---

## Features

The mod currently adds **15+ unique enchantments**, including:

- ⚡ **Shockwave** – Push mobs away when attacking  
- 🔥 **Fire Walker** – Temporarily walk safely on lava  
- 🩸 **Vampiric** – Restores health when dealing damage  
- ⛏ **Excavator** – Break multiple blocks at once  
- 🧲 **Magnetic** – Automatically attracts dropped items  
- 💨 **Double Jump** – Jump again while in the air  
- …and more to come!

Each enchantment has multiple levels with increasing effects, offering depth and variety to your gameplay.

---

## Installation

1. Make sure you have **Minecraft Forge 1.20.1** installed.  
2. Download the latest release of RE:Enchanted from the [Releases](https://github.com/Mysti33/RE-Enchanted/releases) page.  
3. Place the `.jar` file into your `mods` folder.  
4. Launch Minecraft with the Forge profile.
